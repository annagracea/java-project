Emergency Protocols

What to do during a blood emergency.

  1. Call emergency medical services immediately.
  2. Provide patient’s blood group and medical history.
  3. Contact nearest blood bank for urgent supply.
  4. Keep the recipient calm and hydrated.
  5. Ensure donor’s safety during urgent donation.
