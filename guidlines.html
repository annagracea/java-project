Blood Donation Guidelines

Everything you need to know before donating blood.

Drink plenty of water before donating to stay hydrated.

Eat a healthy meal 2–3 hours before donation.

Bring a valid ID card for verification.

Avoid alcohol 24 hours before donating.

Rest for at least 15 minutes after donation.
